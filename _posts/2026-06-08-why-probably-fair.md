In statistics, we rarely know things with absolute certainty.

A coin may be fair.  
A model may be useful.  
An estimator may be consistent.  
A conclusion may be reasonable under assumptions.

That is why I like the name **Probably Fair**.

This blog is my attempt to understand probability, statistics, and mathematical ideas carefully — not perfectly, but honestly.

## What this blog is for

I want this blog to be a place where I can keep notes of what I learned and accomplished, most of which might be about:

- Math notes
- Statistics intuition
- Probability concepts
- Machine learning theory
- Paper summaries
- Research ideas
- Mistakes and lessons learned
