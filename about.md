# About

Hi, I’m Minh Quan.

I study Computer Science and I’m interested in probability, statistics, mathematics, and machine learning.

This blog is my personal learning archive. I use it to write down ideas that I want to understand more deeply.

The goal is not to sound perfect. The goal is to make my thinking clearer.

## Why “Probably Fair”?

In statistics, we often reason under uncertainty.

A coin may be fair.  
An estimator may be consistent.  
A model may be useful.  
A conclusion may be reasonable under assumptions.

But we rarely know things with absolute certainty.

That is why I like the name **Probably Fair**. It sounds playful, but it also captures the spirit of probability and statistics: careful reasoning when certainty is unavailable.
